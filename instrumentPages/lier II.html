Tuning

Nb: a4=440Hz.

Bv. c4-d4-e4-f4-g4-a4 (in the tuner); or pentatonic c4-d4- e4-g4-a4-b4; or c4-d4-e4-g4-a4-c4.

The Prins Claus staff would like to remind you that the above tunings are a recommendation, feel free to experiment with other tunings yourself!
